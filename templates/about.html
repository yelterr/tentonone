About

Ten to None lets you rate the attractiveness of celebrities on a scale of 0-10. I built it because why not. To make it as accurate as possible, I've made all votes anonymous. The images are sourced from all over the internet, and cropped by an AI. Also I'll be adding more images as time goes on, so look out for that.

If you have any questions, requests, or bugs to report please go to the contact us page. If you want to support the project and help keep this data free for everyone, you can buy me a coffee and follow @ten2none on X.
